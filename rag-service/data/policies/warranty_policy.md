# Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty Coverage

All products sold through our store include a manufacturer's warranty. The warranty period varies by product category and is specified on each product's detail page.

## Warranty Periods by Category

### Electronics
- Standard warranty: 12 months from date of purchase
- Extended warranty available: Additional 12 or 24 months (purchased separately)
- Covers: Manufacturing defects, component failures under normal use
- Does not cover: Physical damage, water damage, unauthorized modifications, normal wear and tear

### Portable Storage Devices (SSDs, Hard Drives)
- Standard warranty: 36 months from date of purchase
- Covers: Drive failures, data read/write errors, firmware defects
- Does not cover: Data loss (we recommend regular backups), physical damage from drops

### Home Appliances
- Standard warranty: 12 months from date of purchase
- Extended warranty available: Additional 24 or 48 months
- Covers: Motor failures, electrical component defects, control panel malfunctions
- Does not cover: Cosmetic damage, damage from misuse, consumable parts (filters, brushes)

### Cookware
- Standard warranty: 60 months (5 years) from date of purchase
- Covers: Warping, handle loosening, non-stick coating peeling under normal use
- Does not cover: Discoloration from heat, scratches from metal utensils, dishwasher damage if hand-wash-only

### Clothing and Footwear
- Standard warranty: 6 months from date of purchase
- Covers: Seam failures, zipper defects, material tearing under normal use
- Does not cover: Normal wear, fading from washing, damage from improper care

### Sports Equipment
- Standard warranty: 12-24 months depending on item (see product page)
- Covers: Structural failures, material defects
- Does not cover: Normal wear from use, cosmetic damage

### Books, Toys, and Accessories
- No warranty unless specified on the product page
- Defective items may be returned within 30 days under our standard return policy

## How to File a Warranty Claim

1. Locate your order confirmation email or order number
2. Contact our support team with:
   - Order number
   - Product name and ID
   - Description of the defect or issue
   - Photos or video showing the problem
3. Our team will review the claim within 2 business days
4. If approved, we will provide:
   - A prepaid return shipping label
   - Instructions for returning the defective item
5. Once we receive and verify the defective item, we will:
   - Ship a replacement (same model or equivalent) within 3 business days, OR
   - Issue a full refund if the product is no longer available

## Extended Warranty

Extended warranties can be purchased within 30 days of the original purchase. Extended warranty pricing:

- 12-month extension: 10% of product price
- 24-month extension: 18% of product price
- 48-month extension (appliances only): 30% of product price

Extended warranties include all standard coverage plus:
- Accidental damage protection (one incident per warranty period)
- Free two-way shipping for warranty claims
- Priority processing (replacement shipped within 1 business day)

## Warranty Transfers

Warranties are tied to the original purchaser and are non-transferable. If you purchased an item as a gift, the recipient must provide the original order number to file a claim.

## Limitation of Liability

Our warranty obligation is limited to repair or replacement of the defective product. We are not liable for incidental or consequential damages, loss of use, or any other indirect costs arising from product defects.
