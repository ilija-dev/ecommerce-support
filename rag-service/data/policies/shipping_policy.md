# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Methods and Timeframes

We offer the following shipping options for domestic orders:

### Standard Shipping (Free on orders over $50)
- Estimated delivery: 5-7 business days
- Cost: $5.99 for orders under $50
- Tracking number provided via email within 24 hours of shipment

### Express Shipping
- Estimated delivery: 2-3 business days
- Cost: $12.99
- Tracking number provided via email within 4 hours of shipment

### Overnight Shipping
- Estimated delivery: Next business day (order by 2 PM EST)
- Cost: $24.99
- Available Monday through Friday only
- Not available for PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses

## Order Processing

Orders placed before 2 PM EST Monday through Friday are processed the same business day. Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends/holidays are processed the next business day.

During peak seasons (Black Friday through December 31), processing may take an additional 1-2 business days.

## Shipping Restrictions

- We currently ship to all 50 US states and US territories
- International shipping is not available at this time
- Hazardous materials (lithium batteries shipped alone, aerosols) have shipping restrictions
- Oversized items (over 70 lbs or 130 inches combined dimensions) require freight shipping at additional cost

## Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking email with:
- Carrier name and tracking number
- Estimated delivery date
- Link to track your package in real-time

You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing Order History.

## Delivery Issues

### Package Not Received
If your tracking shows "delivered" but you haven't received your package:
1. Check with neighbors and building management
2. Look for a delivery notice or check alternate delivery locations
3. Wait 24 hours — packages sometimes show delivered before actual arrival
4. Contact us within 48 hours if still not received — we will file a carrier investigation

### Damaged in Transit
If your package arrives damaged:
1. Take photos of the damaged packaging and items
2. Contact us within 72 hours of delivery
3. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund
4. Do not discard the damaged items or packaging until the claim is resolved

### Wrong Item Received
If you received the wrong item:
1. Contact us immediately — do not open or use the incorrect item
2. We will send a prepaid return label and ship the correct item at no charge
3. Express shipping will be used for the replacement at no additional cost

## Holiday Shipping Deadlines

To receive orders by December 25th:
- Standard Shipping: Order by December 14th
- Express Shipping: Order by December 20th
- Overnight Shipping: Order by December 23rd (2 PM EST)

These deadlines are estimates and not guarantees due to carrier volume during the holiday season.
