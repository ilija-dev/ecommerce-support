# Returns Policy

## General Return Window

All items purchased from our store may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided they are in their original condition with all tags and packaging intact. Items returned after 30 days but within 60 days may be eligible for store credit only.

## Electronics Returns

Electronics must be returned within 15 days of delivery. All original packaging, accessories, cables, and manuals must be included. Electronics that have been opened may be subject to a 15% restocking fee. Defective electronics are exempt from the restocking fee and may be returned within the full warranty period.

Serial numbers must match our records. Items with missing or altered serial numbers will not be accepted for return.

## Clothing Returns

Clothing may be returned within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached. Undergarments, swimwear, and personalized items are final sale and cannot be returned.

Shoes may be returned within 30 days if they show no signs of wear. Shoes that have been worn outdoors are not eligible for return. We recommend trying shoes on a clean, indoor surface first.

## Home & Garden Returns

Home and garden items may be returned within 30 days in their original packaging. Assembled furniture cannot be returned unless it arrived damaged or defective. Plants and perishable items are final sale.

Appliances must be returned in their original, unopened packaging for a full refund. Opened appliances may be exchanged for the same item only.

## Books, Toys & Sports Equipment

Books in new condition may be returned within 30 days. Books with highlighting, writing, or damage are not eligible for return.

Toys and sports equipment must be in unopened, original packaging for a full refund. Opened items in like-new condition may be returned for store credit.

## Return Process

1. Log into your account and navigate to Order History
2. Select the order and click "Request Return"
3. Choose your reason for return and preferred refund method
4. Print the prepaid return label (free for defective items, $7.99 deducted from refund for other returns)
5. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location
6. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item

## Exceptions

- Items marked "Final Sale" cannot be returned
- Gift cards are non-refundable
- Customized or personalized items are non-refundable
- Items purchased during clearance events may have a shortened 14-day return window
- Bulk orders (10+ units of the same item) require manager approval for returns
